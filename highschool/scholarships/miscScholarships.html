Miscellaneous Scholarships

Paying for college in completely impractical ways

Ayn Rand Foundation Essay Contest

Who Can Apply Varies. 8th through 12th grade students can apply for the "Anthem" essay contest. 11th and 12th grade students can apply for the "The Fountainhead" essay contest. 12th graders, undergraduates, graduate students can apply for the "Atlas Shrugged" essay contest.
When Do They Apply Varies based on the student's year and essay contest.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an essay.
What Do They Get Varies. In each case, contest winners receive a check to be used for any expenses, not just academic, ranging from $25 to $25,000.
Successful Essay Link.
Website Link aynrand.org/students/essay-contests

Chick Evans Caddie Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school seniors who have caddied for at least two years and have been nominated by their sponsoring club. They need a GPA abov 3.0 and must have taken the ACT and/or SAT. Must demonstrate financial need.
When Do They Apply The application opens on August 1st and closes on October 30th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an application with letter of recommendation, a transcript, standardized test scores, and financial documents.
What Do They Get Full four-year tuition and housing scholarships at certain universities.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link wgaesf.org

Dr. Pepper Tuition Giveaway

Who Can Apply Students aged 18 through 24.
When Do They Apply Varies.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an essay and a video describing how they would change the world.
What Do They Get Up to $100,000 toward college tuition.

Website Link drpeppertuition.com
