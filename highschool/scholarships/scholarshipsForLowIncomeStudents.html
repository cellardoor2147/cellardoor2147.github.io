Scholarships for Low-Income Students

Paying for college from a low-income background

Dell Scholars Program

Who Can Apply Attendees of these CRPs who are eligible for the pell grant and have at least a 2.4 GPA.
When Do They Apply Applications open on October 1st and close on December 1st.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application with a few essays. Semifinialists submit high school transcripts, their FAFSA Student Aid Report (SAR), and a recommendation.
What Do They Get Up to $20,000, a laptop, textbook credits, career counseling, and financial aid counseling.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link dellscholars.org/scholarship

Gates Scholarship (formerly Gates Millennium)

Who Can Apply High school seniors of color who demonstrate eligible for the pell grant and have at least a 3.3 GPA.
When Do They Apply Applications open on July 15th and close on September 18th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application with a high school transcript and community service form. Semifinalists submit an additional online application, essays, an updated transcript, and letters of recommendation. Finalists have an interview.
What Do They Get The full cost of attendence that isn't already covered by other financial aid to any university they can get into.
Successful Essay ngocnhile.wordpress.com/gmsp2
Website Link thegatesscholarship.org/scholarship

Gordon A. Rich Memorial Foundation

Who Can Apply High school seniors with at least a 3.5 GPA who are in the top 20% of their class and demonstrate financial need. Their parents must be full-time employees in the financial services industry.
When Do They Apply Applications open on September 15th and close on February 20th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application with an essay, transcript, and letter of recommendation.
What Do They Get Up to $50,000 and access to GAR scholars network.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link gordonrich.org

Greenhouse Scholars Program

Who Can Apply High school seniors who are residents of Colorado, Georgia, Illinois, or North Carolina. They must have at least a 3.5 GPA, community service interests, and demonstrated financial need.
When Do They Apply Applications open on September 1st and close on December 17th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application. Finalists submit a headshot photo and additional application material before having an in-person interview.
What Do They Get Up to $20,000 and eligibility to apply for $2,000 grants.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link greenhousescholars.org

Hadden Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school seniors with at least a 3.5 GPA and demonstrated financial need.
When Do They Apply Applications open on January 21st and close on February 28th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application with standardized test scores, transcripts, class standings, financial documents, an essay, and two letters of recommendation. Interview may be required.
What Do They Get Up to $16,000.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link fdnweb.org/youthfdn

Horatio Alger National Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school seniors with at least a 2.0 GPA and demonstrated financial need.
When Do They Apply Applications open on August 1st and close on October 25th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application.
What Do They Get $25,000 and an all-expenses paid trip to Washington, D.C. to receive their award.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link scholars.horatioalger.org

Jack Kent Cooke Foundation

Who Can Apply High school seniors with at least a 3.5 GPA, 1200 SAT or 26 ACT composite, and demonstrated financial need.
When Do They Apply Applications open on September 6th and close on November 20th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application via the common app.
What Do They Get Up to $160,000 and access to the JKC scholars network.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link jkcf.org

QuestBridge Scholars Program

Who Can Apply There are no strict cut-offs for QuestBrige. However, finalists generally have primarily A's in the hardest classes available, graduate at the top 5-10% of their class, have SAT/PSAT scores > 1310 and ACT composites > 28, and demonstrate significant financial need.
When Do They Apply Applications open in the Summer and close on September 27th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an application with financial documents, essays, standardized test scores, and letters of recommendation. Finalists may be required to submit additional material if their desired schools require it.
What Do They Get The full cost of attendence that isn't already covered by other financial aid to any QuestBridge university they match to. Additionally, their application fees are waived.
Successful Essay https://sites.google.com/site/qbessays/
Website Link questbridge.org

Who Can Apply High school seniors with at least a 2.0 GPA who demonstrate financial need. They must be a member of an approved non-profit.
When Do They Apply Applications open in Summer and close on November 13rd.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application.
What Do They Get Up to $20,000.
