Scholarships for Students of Color

Paying for college from a minority background

Blacks at Microsoft

Who Can Apply High school seniors of African descent with at least a 3.3 GPA who demonstrate financial need. They must aspire to major in engineering, computer science, computer information, or select business programs.
When Do They Apply Applications close on March 1st.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit a paper aplication.
What Do They Get Up to $20,000 and special consideration for a high school microsoft internship.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link microsoft.com/en-us/diversity/programs/blacks-scholarships

Generation Google Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school seniors of color and university students of color who intend to pursue a degree in computer science, computer engineering, or a degree in a closely related technical field.
When Do They Apply Applications close on December 6th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application with a resume, transcript, a letter of recommendation, and a few essays.
What Do They Get A one-time stipend of $10,000 and an invitation to attend the Google Scholars' Retreat.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link buildyourfuture.withgoogle.com/scholarships/generation-google-scholarship

Hispanic Scholarship Fund

Who Can Apply Hispanic high school seniors with at least a 3.0 GPA and hispanic college students with at least a 2.5 GPA.
When Do They Apply Applications open on January 1st and close on Feburary 15th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application.
What Do They Get A one-time stipend of up to $5,000. Students must re-apply every year.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link www.hsf.net

Flinn Scholars

Who Can Apply Arizona high school seniors. Finalists generally have at least a 3.5 GPA, rank in the top 5% of their class, and have an ACT composite > 29 or SAT score > 1340.
When Do They Apply Applications open in mid-August and close in early-October.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application with a few essays, two letters of recommendation, a report from their high school conselor, a transcript, and standardized test scores.
What Do They Get The full cost of attendence at select Arizona universities, a study abroad summer seminar in China, a mentorship program, and admissions to their university's honor college.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link flinn.org

Posse Foundation Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school seniors living in Atlanta, the Bay Area, Boston, Chicago, Houston, Los Angeles, Miami, New Orleans, New York, or Washington, D.C who are nominated by their school or community-based organization.
When Do They Apply Varies.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application. Quad-finalists, semi-finalists, and finalists all have an interview and submit academic materials.
What Do They Get The full cost of tuition to a Posse Foundation partner school, admission to pre-college workshops, and access to the Posse scholars network.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link possefoundation.org

Stamps Foundation Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school seniors with at least a 3.85 GPA and an SAT score > 1300 or ACT composite > 28.
When Do They Apply Varies.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application to their Stamps affiliate university.
What Do They Get Up to $275,000.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link stampsfoundation.org
