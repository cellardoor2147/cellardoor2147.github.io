Merit Scholarships

Paying for college using past successes

Coca-Cola Scholars Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school seniors with at least a 3.0 GPA.
When Do They Apply Applications open on August 1st and close on October 31st.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application with a transcript. No essays or additional materials are required.
What Do They Get A one-time stipend of $20,000.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link coca-colascholarsfoundation.org/

Coolidge Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school juniors.
When Do They Apply Applications open on October 19 and close on January 23rd.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application. Finalists have an in-person interview.
What Do They Get The full cost of attendence that isn't already covered by other financial aid to any university they can get into.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link coolidgescholars.org/

Elks Most Valuable Student Competition

Who Can Apply High school seniors who have taken the ACT or SAT.
When Do They Apply Applications open on August 15th and close on November 15th.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application. Semifinalists submit additional application material, an essay, and a letter of recommendation. Finalists have an in-person interview.
What Do They Get Up to $50,000.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link elks.org/scholars/scholarships/mvs

Flinn Scholars

Who Can Apply Arizona high school seniors. Finalists generally have at least a 3.5 GPA, rank in the top 5% of their class, and have an ACT composite > 29 or SAT score > 1340.
When Do They Apply Applications open in mid-August and close in early-October.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application with a few essays, two letters of recommendation, a report from their high school conselor, a transcript, and standardized test scores.
What Do They Get The full cost of attendence at select Arizona universities, a study abroad summer seminar in China, a mentorship program, and admissions to their university's honor college.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link flinn.org

Posse Foundation Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school seniors living in Atlanta, the Bay Area, Boston, Chicago, Houston, Los Angeles, Miami, New Orleans, New York, or Washington, D.C who are nominated by their school or community-based organization.
When Do They Apply Varies.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application. Quad-finalists, semi-finalists, and finalists all have an interview and submit academic materials.
What Do They Get The full cost of tuition to a Posse Foundation partner school, admission to pre-college workshops, and access to the Posse scholars network.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link possefoundation.org

Stamps Foundation Scholarship

Who Can Apply High school seniors with at least a 3.85 GPA and an SAT score > 1300 or ACT composite > 28.
When Do They Apply Varies.
How Do They Apply All applicants submit an online application to their Stamps affiliate university.
What Do They Get Up to $275,000.
Successful Essay N/A
Website Link stampsfoundation.org
